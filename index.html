Play the greatest RPG ever known to mankind.

Knight

Knights have high health and stamina, however their strength also makes them clumsier.

Rogue

Rogues are fast and have high stamina and speed which makes up for their lack of strength and health.

Wizard

Wizards put all their effort casting spells using their glass canons. This makes them slack on everything else.

Hunter

Hunters are well rounded fighters that focus on everning out their skills. No strength or weaknesses.
